Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 10, 2026

At Maachbazar, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

Data We Collect

  • We collect your name, phone, and address for order fulfillment. We need these details to confirm your orders and deliver fresh fish to your doorstep.

Data Sharing

  • We may share order details with local sellers/delivery partners. This is necessary to facilitate the delivery of your order. We share only the information required for delivery.
  • We do not sell your data. Your personal information is never sold to third parties for marketing or other purposes.

Data Retention & Deletion

  • Data is stored for 90 days and then deleted. We retain order history and conversation logs for 90 days to assist with customer support and order issues. After this period, personal data is anonymized or deleted.
  • You can request deletion by emailing support@maachbazar.com. If you wish to have your data deleted sooner, please contact us, and we will process your request promptly.
